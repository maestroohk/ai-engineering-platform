## 3. Zero deviations

The M3 closeout follows the Milestone
Closeout Standard as-is (the standard is
mature enough to be reused without
modification; the M2.6 closeout's
"introduce the standard" is amortised).
The M3 closeout mirrors the M2.6 closeout's
structure with M3-specific evidence; the
M3 retrospective mirrors the M2
retrospective's structure with M3-specific
evidence.

The M3 closeout's deviations from the M2.6
closeout template are explicitly recorded
in the M3 retrospective's § 0
(introductory note) and § 1 (Delivered
capabilities) and § 6 (Architecture
changes) and § 8 (Testing summary) and
§ 11 (Commit range). The deviations are
*content* deviations (M3 has different
evidence from M2), not *process*
deviations (the closeout procedure is
identical).
